Plec is a sustainable exhibition furniture designed for children between 4 and 12 years old. Starting from two different styles, Brutalism and Surrealism, this design was realized.




As far as the concept is concerned, we started from typical characteristics that can be found in both styles. Further research showed that geometric shapes were indispensable. The use of lines as a structure is also very striking.


From this we could further conclude that we wanted to develop a modular concept. This involves working around one central volume in which the other additional volumes should fit. In this way we have come to very block-like, geometric and repetitive shapes.

After we had laid down our concept, it was important to incorporate all parts of the schedule of requirements. A volume study was conducted to determine which functions we would integrate into the different volumes.
